Yifeng Xu

Hi~ Thank you for dropping by! I am a M.S. student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a concentration on Robotics. I am broadly interested in perceptive and complex robotic systems, spanning manipulators, humanoids, and beyond. My ultimate goal is to enable adaptive machine intelligence across diverse embodiments, objects, and environments. My research aims to develop robots that provide safe, reliable, and interactive services for human-centered applications.

I am currently interested in robot simulation and Sim2Real transfer, which I believe will play a key role in the future of next-generation robotics. If you find our research a potential match, feel free to reach out via email!
