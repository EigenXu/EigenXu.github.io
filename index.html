Yifeng Xu

Hi~ Thank you for dropping by! I am a M.S. student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a concentration on Robotics. I am broadly interested in robotic manipulation, spanning perception, control, and action, with the ultimate goal of enabling adaptive manipulation across diverse end-effectors, objects, and environments. My research aims to develop robots that provide safe, reliable, and interactive services for human-centered applications.

I am currently interested in robot simulation and Sim2Real transfer, which I believe will play a key role in the future of next-generation robotics. If you find our research a potential match, feel free to reach out via email!
